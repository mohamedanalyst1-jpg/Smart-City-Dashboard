# 🌍 Smart Cities Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes smart city performance across multiple global cities using key indicators related to citizen satisfaction, safety, sustainability, infrastructure investment, and renewable energy initiatives.

The dashboard helps decision-makers identify top-performing cities, compare regional performance, and monitor smart city development trends.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Monitor overall smart city performance.
- Identify the highest-ranking city.
- Compare smart city scores across regions.
- Analyze citizen satisfaction trends over time.
- Evaluate infrastructure investment distribution.
- Measure renewable energy budget allocation.
- Support data-driven urban planning decisions.

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI | Description |
|------|------------|
| Avg Smart Score | Overall average smart city score |
| Top City | Best-performing city |
| Total Cities | Number of analyzed cities |
| Avg Smart Safety Score | Average safety score across cities |

---

## 📊 Dashboard Components

### 1. Citizen Satisfaction Trend
Tracks the average citizen satisfaction percentage across years to identify long-term trends.

### 2. Smart City Index Map
Interactive global map showing the distribution of smart city scores across different locations.

### 3. Infrastructure Investment Analysis
Treemap visualization displaying total infrastructure investment by city.

### 4. Renewable Energy Budget Analysis
Bar chart comparing renewable energy budget allocation across cities.

### 5. Regional Filter
Allows users to dynamically filter the dashboard by region.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Singapore achieved the highest overall smart city performance.
- Citizen satisfaction peaked during 2021.
- Significant infrastructure investments are concentrated in major metropolitan cities.
- Renewable energy spending varies considerably across cities.
- Smart city development is strongly linked to sustainability initiatives.
